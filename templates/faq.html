General Questions

What is UltraVid?

UltraVid is a free online video downloader that allows you to download videos from popular social media platforms like TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram. Our service is completely free, requires no registration, and prioritizes your privacy and security.

Is UltraVid really free?

Yes, UltraVid is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, premium subscriptions, or payment requirements. We believe in providing accessible video downloading services to everyone.

Do I need to register or create an account?

No registration required! You can use UltraVid immediately without creating an account or providing any personal information. Simply paste the video URL and start downloading.

Download Process

How do I download a video?

Downloading is simple: 1) Copy the video URL from TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram, 2) Paste it into our download box, 3) Click the download button, 4) Choose your preferred quality, and 5) Your video will be downloaded automatically.

What video quality will I get?

UltraVid automatically downloads videos in the highest quality available from the source. This could be 480p, 720p HD, 1080p Full HD, or even 4K Ultra HD, depending on the original video's resolution.

Can I download audio only?

Yes! UltraVid supports audio extraction. You can download just the audio track in MP3 format, perfect for music videos or podcasts.

Technical & Troubleshooting

Why isn't my download working?

Common issues include: invalid URLs, private videos, age-restricted content, or platform updates. Make sure the video is public and the URL is correct. If problems persist, try refreshing the page or contact our support.

What devices are supported?

UltraVid works on all devices with a web browser - Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, Android, and tablets. Our responsive design ensures a great experience on any screen size.

How fast are downloads?

Download speeds depend on your internet connection and the video size. Our servers are optimized for fast processing, typically taking 10-30 seconds for most videos.

Legal & Safety

Is downloading videos legal?

Downloading videos for personal use is generally legal, but you should respect copyright laws and platform terms of service. Always ensure you have permission to download content and don't redistribute copyrighted material.

Is UltraVid safe to use?

Yes, UltraVid is completely safe. We use secure HTTPS connections, don't store your data, and regularly scan for malware. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

Do you store downloaded videos?

No, we don't store any videos on our servers. Videos are processed temporarily and automatically deleted after download. We respect your privacy and don't keep any personal data.
