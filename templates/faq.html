Download Process & Features

How do I download a TikTok video step by step?

Follow these simple steps to download any TikTok video:

  1. 1
    Find your video: Open TikTok and navigate to the video you want to download. Make sure it's publicly accessible.
  2. 2
    Copy the URL: Tap the "Share" button and select "Copy Link" or copy the URL from your browser's address bar.
  3. 3
    Paste and download: Return to UltraVid, paste the URL in the input field, and click "Download."
  4. 4
    Wait for processing: Our system will analyze the video and prepare it for download (usually takes 5-10 seconds).
  5. 5
    Save your video: The download will start automatically, and the file will be saved to your device's download folder.

Pro tip: For the best results, ensure you have a stable internet connection and that the TikTok video is fully loaded before copying the URL.
