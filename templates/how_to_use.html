Platform-Specific Guides

How to Download TikTok Videos

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. 1 Open TikTok app or website and find the video you want to download
  2. 2 Tap the "Share" button (arrow icon) on the right side of the video
  3. 3 Select "Copy Link" from the sharing options
  4. 4 Come to UltraVid and paste the link in the download box
  5. 5 Click "Download" and video will process in highest quality

Important Notes:

  • Downloads videos without watermark
  • Supports HD quality when available
  • Works with public videos only
  • Private videos cannot be downloaded
  • Respect creators' copyright

Tips & Best Practices

For Best Results:

  • Use the full URL: Always copy the complete video URL for better compatibility
  • Stable internet: Ensure you have a stable internet connection for smooth downloads
  • Be patient: Large or high-quality videos may take longer to process
  • Public videos only: Only public videos can be downloaded

Quality Options:

  • 480p Good for mobile viewing and smaller file sizes
  • 720p HD quality, perfect balance of quality and size
  • 1080p Full HD for crisp, clear viewing
  • 4K Ultra HD for maximum quality (when available)

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Download Failed or Error Message

  • • Check if the video URL is correct and complete
  • • Ensure the video is public and not private/restricted
  • • Try refreshing the page and attempting again
  • • Wait a few minutes and retry (temporary server issues)

Slow Download Speeds

  • • Check your internet connection speed
  • • Try downloading during off-peak hours
  • • Choose a lower quality option for faster downloads
  • • Close other bandwidth-intensive applications

Video Quality Issues

  • • Higher quality options depend on the original video quality
  • • Some platforms compress videos, limiting available qualities
  • • Try different quality options to find the best available
  • • Original quality is always preserved when possible
